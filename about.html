About Company

Hola,

Our company (Gaming Hub) started it’s product (Gamempire) back in 2012. The idea was created from a competitive gamer who was sick of the online hassle of finding information and softwares to satisfy his gaming needs. Once the idea was created to centralize and customize that to each gamer we had our idea.

The next summer we earned our “Summer Company Grant”, which was a government funded, entrepreneurial program to give students the fundamentals and knowledge of beginning their own startup. From that grant we earned the required funds to start our own website allowing gamers to create and customize their own tournaments and a streaming page where users could resize their viewing experience to their liking.

Now, in September 2016, we have hired a full team of capable staff to build our full idea of Gamempire from a website to an application. Our goal is to have a fully functional prototype by the end of October. Our goal for the next year is to be presenting on the E3 stage on June 9th, 2017 and in the next 3 years be the #1 gaming application in the world. We recognize the current market potential and are very excited with our product moving forward.
